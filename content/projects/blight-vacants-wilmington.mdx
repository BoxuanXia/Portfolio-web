## Overview
This project visualized the blight and vacant properties in the city of Wilmington. Using the ArcGIS Online dashboard, I created a real-time monitor for urban planners.

## Impact
Enabled city officials to prioritize demolition and rehabilitation efforts based on spatial clustering of vacant properties.
